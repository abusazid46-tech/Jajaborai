About Jajabor AI Innovations

Pioneering AI and digital solutions from the heart of Assam

Our Story

Founded in Guwahati, Assam, Jajabor AI Innovations Pvt Ltd is on a mission to bring cutting-edge technology to Northeast India. We specialize in mobile app development, website development, and AI solutions with a special focus on Assamese language technology.

Our Founders

Abu Sazid Ahmed (B.Tech Electronics & Telecommunication) and Mustafur Rahman (B.E Mechanical) started this journey to make AI accessible for Assamese-speaking communities.

Our Vision

To establish Assam as a hub for AI innovation and digital transformation in Northeast India.
